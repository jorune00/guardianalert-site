Guardian Alert

Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 21, 2026

Guardian Alert is a personal safety check-in app that helps users schedule check-ins and notify trusted contacts if a check-in is missed.

Information We Collect

Guardian Alert may collect and store information needed to provide its core features, including:

  • Account email address used for sign-in
  • Trusted contact email addresses
  • Contact consent status and related records
  • Check-in schedule settings
  • Escalation preferences
  • Check-in activity and alert status
  • Basic service and delivery metadata needed to operate the app reliably and securely

How We Use Information

  • Provide scheduled safety check-in functionality
  • Send alert emails when a user misses a check-in
  • Manage trusted contact consent
  • Maintain app reliability, functionality, and security
  • Improve the performance and usability of the service

Contact Consent

Guardian Alert is designed to notify only trusted contacts selected by the user. Contacts must consent before they are activated for alerts.

Sharing of Information

We do not sell personal information.

Information may be shared only as needed to operate the service, such as with providers used for:

  • App hosting and backend infrastructure
  • Email delivery
  • Domain, networking, and related operational services

Data Retention

We retain information only as long as reasonably necessary to provide the service, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements.

Data Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures to protect personal information. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

Your Choices

Users may update or remove certain account, schedule, or contact information through the app or by contacting support. Users may delete their account in the app from Settings → Delete account, or by following the instructions on our Account Deletion page.

Children’s Privacy

Guardian Alert is not directed to children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updated versions will be posted at the current policy URL with a revised effective date.
