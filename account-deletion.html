Guardian Alert

Account Deletion

Effective date: April 30, 2026

Guardian Alert users can request deletion of their account and associated data at any time.

Delete your account in the app

The fastest way to delete your account is inside Guardian Alert:

  1. Open the Guardian Alert app.
  2. Sign in to your account if needed.
  3. Open Settings.
  4. Select Delete account.
  5. Confirm the deletion prompt.

When confirmed, Guardian Alert deletes your account from the service and signs you out of the app.

Request deletion by email

If you cannot access the app, email guardianalert.app@agentmail.to from the email address associated with your Guardian Alert account and ask us to delete your account.

We may need to verify that you control the account email address before completing an email-based deletion request.

Data deleted

Account deletion removes data associated with your Guardian Alert account, including:

  • Your Guardian Alert account record and sign-in sessions
  • Check-in schedule settings and escalation preferences
  • Trusted contact records and contact consent status
  • Check-in activity, alert status, and related service records tied to your account

Data we may retain

We may retain limited operational, security, delivery, backup, or legal records only as reasonably necessary to operate the service, protect the service, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, or enforce agreements. Retained records are not used to keep your deleted account active.

Questions

For deletion, privacy, or support questions, contact: guardianalert.app@agentmail.to

Guardian Alert is a personal safety and accountability tool. It is not a replacement for emergency services.
